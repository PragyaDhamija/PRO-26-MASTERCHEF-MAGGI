***Ingredients for making "Lemony MAGGI Noodles":***

* MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
        1 Pack

* Lemon Juice
        1 tablespoon

* Oil
        1 tablespoon

* Mustard Seeds
        ¼ teaspoon

* Urad Dal
        ½ teaspoon

* Chana Dal
        ½ teaspoon

* Green Chillies
        1 chopped

* Curry Leaves
        8-10 leaves

* Asafoetida Powder (Hing)
        A pinch

* Roasted Peanuts
        2 tablespoons

* Coriander Leaves
        2 tablespoons, chopped
